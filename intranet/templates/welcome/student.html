TJ Email Accounts and Network Use

All students at TJHSST are assigned their own email address for school purposes. This address is in the format [yourusername]@tjhsst.edu.

Your TJ email address is: {{ request.user.tj_email }}

You can access your "TJ email" by going to webmail.tjhsst.edu. You can also use a desktop mail client such as Thunderbird to access your email through IMAP or POP3. Your TJ email credentials are the same as your Intranet credentials, as well as the username and password you use to log in to school computers.

If you primarily use a personal email account, it is strongly recommended that you set up email forwarding, which will ensure that you receive all of the messages sent to your account. Follow these instructions to set this up. If you have any questions about this setup process, email ahamilto@tjhsst.edu.

Use of TJHSST network resources is governed by the Fairfax County Public Schools Acceptable Use Policy for Student Network Access, part of the FCPS SR&R, as well as the TJHSST Network User Guidelines. TJHSST does not (currently) abide by the FCPS BYOD system; no device registration is necessary. Please use network resources responsibly.
